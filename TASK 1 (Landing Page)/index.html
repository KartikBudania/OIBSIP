Anshul Jubli (born 13 January 1995) also known as King of Lions is an Indian mixed martial artist from New Delhi who is currently signed to the lightweight division of Ultimate Fighting Championship, where he became the second person from India, to get a UFC contract, as well as first person from India to win in a UFC fight, and the second person from India to fight in the UFC, after Bharat Khandare.
